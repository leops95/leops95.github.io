State of the State transcripts

Plain text database at the U.S. state level, yearly

Jul 26, 2020
Image credit: Flickr

Usually held at the beginning of each year, U.S. governors give a public speech where they talk about the situation of their state. Frequency of the speech varies from state to state, as it is sometimes held every two year, or ‘from time to time’. They can be supported by an inauguration speech after an election year, or a state of the budget speech.

You will soon be able to find the data on my GitHub repository.
